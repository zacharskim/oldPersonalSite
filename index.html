About

Hi! I'm Matt. I'm a senior statistics major from Cincinnati, OH.

I'm passionate about creating, influencing, aligning private incentives to benefit the public. Basically, I think the business world is able to bring about positive changes in society, with some corporations already doing so. However, I think the business world can do better. I'm trying to find out how to make that happen.

Some of my favorite books/articles: The Undoing Project, Thinking, Fast and Slow, Procrastination, Principles

Hobbies
